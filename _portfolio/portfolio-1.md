The OMIA Workshop and Ophthalmic Image Analysis Challenge serve as our annual platform for advancing AI research in ophthalmology. Held alongside the MICCAI conference, these events release annotated datasets tailored to specific tasks. We warmly invite you to learn more. For details, visit the [HDMI Lab-OMIA Page](http://hdmilab.cn/omia).
